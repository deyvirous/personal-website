Alienated

Artist: Zayn Malik
Genre: Alternative/ Indie
Release Year: 2024

"I was just feeling alienated at the time in certain situations. That was kind of the sentiment behind the whole idea of the song, so that's why I felt it fit as the title. And I explain in the song exactly why I'm feeling that way, how I have dealt with those situations, and how it's felt in that place." - Zayn.
